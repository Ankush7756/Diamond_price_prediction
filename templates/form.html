Diamond Price Prediction

The Diamond Price Prediction project utilizes machine learning to forecast the prices of diamonds based on their key attributes such as carat weight, cut, color, clarity, and dimensions. By preprocessing the data, training various machine learning models, and evaluating their performance using metrics like mean absolute error and R-squared, this project aims to provide a reliable tool for estimating diamond prices in the market.
